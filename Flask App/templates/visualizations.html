Age & Gender

Most deaths occured in patients ages 71-80 years old. 

Female patients are more likely to survive based on this dataset.  

Ethinicity & Survival Rate

The majority of the patients in the data set are Caucasian, so this group makes up the most deaths and survivors.  

The other ethnicities have <1% death rate. 

 Deaths by body system & Average Age

Cardiovasular patients have the highest death count with an average patient age of 65.95.

Gynecological patients have the lowest death count.

Impact of GSC & Ventilators

The Glasgow Coma Scale assesses a person's level of consciousness when they enter the hospital or ICU. Most patients that ultimately died, came into the hospital alert & oriented and then eventually required mechanical ventilation.

ICU Type & death Rate

Most patient deaths occur when a patient is admitted into the Med-Surg or Medical-Surgical ICU. 
*Of note,it is common practice for hospitals have patients on different units despite their diagnosis due to bed availability and census.
